Md. Farhad Momin is a civil engineering academic and researcher with a focus on structural engineering, earthquake engineering, and performance assessment of reinforced concrete buildings. His work mainly involves seismic evaluation of existing structures, structural modeling, and practical assessment of building performance under seismic loading.

His research experience includes seismic vulnerability assessment of RC buildings, comparison of seismic assessment approaches, and studies related to structural parameters influencing building response. He has also contributed to research on concrete materials, particularly recycled aggregate concrete, and has a growing interest in data driven methods as supportive tools in civil engineering research. Alongside research, he teaches undergraduate civil engineering courses and guides students in structural analysis, design, modeling, experimental work, and technical writing.
